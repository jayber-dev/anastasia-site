overview

Data management tools such as CRM and ERP are essential for managing customer,
financial, and other business data. Approximately 90% of organizations with 11 or more employees use a CRM, which explains why the ERP market is expected to exceed $49.5 billion in just a few years. To make the most of both platforms and ensure that your most critical business processes function effectively, you must integrate them.

The purpose of a customer relationship management platform is to help employees manage customer relationships. Employees in sales, support, marketing, and customer success frequently use it. To use this platform, every team member can access essential client information, add information (for example, contact information or notes about a recent interaction), upload files, build reports, or take action (for example, sending emails). On the other hand, ERPs are software programs that allow organizations to perform various day-to- day tasks, most of which are sure to finance. Any activities related to accounting, procurement, risk management, and supply chain management fall under this category.

The Challenge

Redesigning the Engine platform to make it faster, simpler, and visually appealing would benefit users. The customer can create a customized workflow and integrate and automate all his processes in one place.

The Audience

There are two primary target audiences that Engini appeals to:

1. Small companies that might require a generic solution for a specific problem in management, sales process, inventory management, administration tasks, etc.

2 . Those who are assisted by an system implementor for the benefit of characterization and implementation. Business process management systems

Research

Many players in the market offer platforms that help you automate no-code workflows between your favorite apps and other services like synchronizing files, triggering notifications, collecting data, and more. My role was to conduct research to identify our competitors and figure out what we could do to create a better platform. What People Use Today : Zappier, Workato, Make, Power Automate by Microsoft, and more Conclusion Bringing ERP and CRM together removes data silos between sales and finance, allows employees to (more easily) avoid human errors and enables sales reps and accounts receivable specialists to focus on more strategic, business- critical tasks.

Pain Points

Before we started to develop the new system with the Product manager, we tried to understand all the pain points of the Engini platform. 1. Platform was very complicated and twisty. 2. The design was unclear. Different font and size were used, and there was no matching pattern. 3. The software contained too much irrelevant information. 4. The Solution

Engini is a no-code platform for building apps and workflow automation on top of ERPs & SaaS. It is meant to provide users with simplified solutions which allow for incorporating Business Processes, Create a customized workflow, Integration & Automation and everything on one platform. Time is money: Our customers understand this universal value which is why they will turn to our platform to help their Businesses get more time out of their day.

With Engini's product, both audiences Business takes a big step forward For a Small company needing a solution, the significant jump can be a template set of Solutions that customers install utilizing our product and get value immediately. For Medium customers and above, Engini can provide a substantial jump by serving as a Development instrument for the system implementors, and they are those who will receive value from the product
